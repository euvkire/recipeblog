Fried Rice for beginners

I am still working on the taste but it might taste good for you if you try it But add a little less salt, but you dont have to make the fried rice since its sort of a prototype of me trying to make one of the best fried rice in the world!

Equipment

Bamboo folding thingy, bowl, whisk,

Ingredients

Cilantro, Rice, Shrimp, Garlic, Salt, Pepper, Oyster sauce, Sweet Soy Sauce, Green Onions, Cubed Carrots, Green Peas, and Eggs

Steps!!

Step 1: Cut Cilantro, and Green Onions

Step 2: Start heating the oil in a Pan

Step 3: Crack the eggs open and add in a teaspoon of salt and pepper into the eggs and start mixing it.

Step 4: After the oil is ready add in the Eggs and rice and start adding both of them together.

Step 5: Add in the Cilantro, Green Peas, Green Onions, and your Cubed Carrots and Shrimp, but the Shrimp has to have the shells off of it.

Step 6: Add in your sweet soy sauce and oyster sauce.

Step 7: After 3-5 Minutes it should be done cooking!

Step 8: Serve the dish!
