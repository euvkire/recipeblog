How To Make A Bowl Of Cereal

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

  • Bowl
  • Spoon

Step 2: Gather Your Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Cereal

Step 3: Get Bowl And Add Cereal

  1. First get your bowl and place it on the counter top or table
  2. Then add cereal

Step 4: Add Milk

  1. Get milk
  2. Remove cap
  3. Pour onto cereal
  4. Put cap back on milk

Step 5: Add Spoon And Enjoy!

  1. Finally add your spoon and enjoy your bowl of cereal
