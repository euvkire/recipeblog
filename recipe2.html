How To Make A Bowl Of Noodles

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

  • Bowl
  • Fork

Step 2: Gather Your Ingredients

  • Cup of noodles
  • Water

Step 3: Get Your cup of noodles and add water to the noodles

  1. First get your cup noodles open it and hold it under a sink
  2. Then Add the water to the noodles

Step 4: put it in a MICROWAVE

  1. put the noodles in the microwave
  2. put it around 3 minutes
  3. wait for the timer to finish
  4. then get your cup of noodles

Step 5: Add your fork and ENJOY!

  1. Finally add your fork to your cup of noodles
